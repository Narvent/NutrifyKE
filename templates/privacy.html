Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

NutrifyKE ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by NutrifyKE.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, log meals, or upload images for analysis. This includes:

  • Email address (for account management)
  • Food logs and nutritional data
  • Images uploaded for AI analysis (processed temporarily)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Analyze food images to estimate nutritional content.
  • Track your daily nutritional progress.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no security system is impenetrable and we cannot guarantee the security of our systems 100%.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@nutrifyke.com.
